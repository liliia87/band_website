**Peaceful people**

The "Peaceful people" is a teen band. This band were created in 2014. All of them are imigrants form difernt countries. They came to US as a refuges becouse a war in their countries. They met in refuges camp near Seattle. This band consist form 5 people. One of them is a great singer. Also there are two drummers, one guitarist and one bassist. From the beginning they realise that to be a great band, they need to make sure each individual has a desire to play in. The band consists of two twins brother, they are playing on drummers.
